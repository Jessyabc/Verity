# Verity Monitor — product framing (V1)

## ICP

Investors and researchers who want **official company sources** (IR, press, filings-linked documents) with **source-linked** context — not a terminal, not social, not trading advice.

## Disclaimers (product copy)

- Informational only; **not investment advice**.
- AI-assisted text must stay **grounded** in fetched or parsed primary materials.
- **Coverage is explicit**: users see what is monitored and what is blocked (e.g. robots.txt).

## Pilot universe

V1 ships with a **hand-curated** list in code (`src/data/pilot-universe.ts`). Expand toward **10–30** issuers with real IR mappings before promising broad coverage.

## Positioning

Calm, trustworthy monitoring — **reliable change detection** (new URLs / optional hash) over “smart” noisy diffs.
